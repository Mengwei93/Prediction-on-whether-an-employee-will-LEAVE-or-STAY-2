Frequently Asked Questions


Who would you recommend to use this APP?

Unless otherwise stated in our description, anyone can use this APP for study, working or even just for fun. However, generally, we do recommend senior managers in company to use this APP for analyzing the future possible employee turnover within their own department.

When can I get the report of evaluation and prediction?

Instantly! Due to our high-efficient algorithm, our app can show you the results just several seconds after you upload your employee data.

Any requirement format for my personal dataset?

Sure, currently, we only accept CSV file and the format of the data you upload should be in line with our train data.

Can you promise information security?

We are committed to our policy of protecting the rights and privacy of our users. So we assure you that we will not transfer your data to any third party. We will not use your data for others purpose other than those agreed by you.
